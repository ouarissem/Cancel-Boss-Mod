This simple mod adds a new utility item called "Cancel Boss".  
When used, it will immediately cancel (despawn) any active boss in the world,  
including bosses from other mods like Calamity, Thorium, and more.  

Crafting recipe:  
- 2 Iron Bars or Lead Bars
- 10 Stone Blocks  
(at a Work Bench)  

Perfect for testing, building, or simply if you want to stop a boss fight instantly.
